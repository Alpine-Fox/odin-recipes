Queso

Required Ingredients

  • 1oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup extra sharp cheddar
  • 1/4 cup extra heavy whipping cream

(Optional) Extras and Spices

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Diced Jalapeños
  • Diced Tomatoes

The Recipe

  1. Melt cream cheese so it gets soft enough to smear with a silicon spatula.
  2. Very slowly add cream until you have a good texture going.
  3. Gradually add cheddar and stir as it melts.
  4. (Optional) Add your perferred extras and spices.
  5. Pour queso into glass container, and add diced tomatoes on-top.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
