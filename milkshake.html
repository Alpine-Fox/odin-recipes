Milkshake

Required Ingredients

  • 8oz unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • 4oz crushed ice
  • Keto protein powder

(Optional) Mix-ins

  • 0 net-carb carmel
  • Catilina Crunch
  • Peanut butter
  • Low-sugar fruits

The Recipe

  1. Measure out your ingredients, each into separate bowls or containers.
  2. Pour your almond milk into the container with the crushed ice.
  3. Scoop in your protien powder mix (I prefer 1-2 scoops).
  4. (Optional) Include your preferred mix-ins.
  5. Blend until there are no chunks of ice.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
